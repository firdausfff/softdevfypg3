    About Us

In the sea of upcoming gigs and local concerts held by independent organizers inspired after seeing the plethora of international concerts coming to Malaysia and Singapore, such as The Eras Tour by Taylor Swift in Singapore , and Coldplay : Music of the spheres , hosted at our very own Stadium Nasional Bukit Jalil.

We recognize the passion with local event organizers willing to work with their Do-It-Yourself style (DIY), having to jot down every single name, payment, and booking by hand. While we respect the effort and willingness to work with whatever resources they have, the GoGig team sees an opportunity to help.

GoGig is a ticket booking/selling website looking to support independant concert/gig organizers who have issues with having a safe and secure website to handle their transactions..

All payments will be done on the website where we enable local gig organizers to advertise their events here.

We provide an easy and reliable way for concert-goers to ensure that their booking won't be lost in the sea of payments and name-logging that the organizers have to do.
